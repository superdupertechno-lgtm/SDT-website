THE ORIGIN, IN FULL

From talking about worlds to building them.

Super Duper Techno started as a YouTube channel in 2016 — gaming and superhero commentary, a place to talk about other people's worlds. That turned into a drive to build one of its own: not a copy, not a formula, something that actually felt like SDT's. It grew into a studio spanning games, software, hardware, media, and original storytelling — eight divisions, one signal. Every product still runs on the principle it started with: creativity and fun over standard edition.

WHAT ACTUALLY GUIDES THE WORK

Core values.

Creativity first

Every idea starts from imagination, not imitation. If it's been done before, SDT looks for the angle that hasn't.

Fun-driven

Fun is the design brief, not an afterthought. If a project stops being fun to build, it's not being built right.

Original by default

Own characters, own universe, own tech. Nothing here is a copy — everything is made to stand on its own.

Unashamedly bold

A shared universe, an in-house engine, original hardware — treated as the plan, not as "too ambitious."
